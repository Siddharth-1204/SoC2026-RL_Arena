## Assignment

Build a simple grid-based simulation where a player moves inside a 2D board.

### Create the Grid (3 x 10)

Example:

. . . . . . . . . .  
. . . . . . . . . .  
. . . . . . . . . .  

### Add Player
Represent the player using `P`.

### Movement

- Take movement input using: `W A S D`
- Update player position accordingly

### Boundary Checking

- Prevent the player from leaving the grid
- If an invalid move is made, print: `"Invalid Move"`

### Obstacles

- Add at least 5 obstacle cells represented by `X`
- The player should not move through obstacles
